# Essential Amenities

## Unit essentials
- 1 bedroom layout or better
- In-unit washer/dryer
- Dishwasher
- Central A/C and heat
- Modern kitchen and bath finish level
- Good natural light
- Acceptable closet/storage
- Cat-friendly lease terms

## Building essentials
- Secure package handling
- Reliable maintenance
- Secure access / controlled entry
- Acceptable parking solution
- Gym
- Clean, well-maintained common areas
- Professional leasing/management responsiveness

## Lifestyle essentials
- Quiet enough for normal daily life
- Stable building culture
- Adult / grown-up community feel
- Close enough to preferred Hollywood / West Hollywood orbit
