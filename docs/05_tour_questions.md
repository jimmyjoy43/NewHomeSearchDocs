# Tour Questions

Bring this list to every tour. Ask every question. Note the answer and
whether it felt direct, evasive, or rehearsed. The quality of the answer
matters as much as the content.

---

## A. Before the tour starts (leasing office)

1. How long does application approval typically take from submission?
   - If > 7 business days: flag. May be off the list given timeline.
2. Is this the exact unit I would be signing for, or a model/comparable?
3. What is the all-in monthly cost? (base + parking + pet rent + utilities estimate)
4. What fees are due at signing? (application fee, admin fee, deposit)
5. What concessions are currently available and when do they expire?
6. How is net effective rent calculated if you are offering free months?
7. Is the building currently under any active construction or renovation?
   - If yes: what areas, what timeline, what noise hours?

---

## B. During the unit walkthrough

8. What floor is this unit on and what does it face? (street, courtyard, alley)
9. Are the windows double-pane or single-pane?
10. Where is the nearest elevator, stairwell, and garbage room relative to this unit?
11. Is there a unit above? Below? What is the floor/ceiling construction type?
12. What is the HVAC system - central air or through-wall units?
13. Is there a balcony or patio? What does it face and what is the noise level?
14. Show me the in-unit washer/dryer hookup or the included unit.
15. Where is parking located? Is my spot assigned or unassigned?
16. Is there a storage unit included or available?

---

## C. Building operations

17. Who do I contact for maintenance requests - a portal, phone, or in person?
18. What is the average response time for non-emergency maintenance?
19. Is there 24-hour emergency maintenance coverage?
20. How is package delivery handled? Is there a staffed desk or automated system?
21. Is there a property manager on site daily? What are their hours?
22. How long has the current management team been at this property?
23. What is the guest parking situation?

---

## D. Community and building culture

24. What is the resident mix like - mostly long-term tenants, short-term, or students?
25. Are there quiet hours enforced? How are noise complaints handled?
26. Has there been any criminal activity or security incidents in the past year?
27. Are there any known issues with the building I should know about?
   - Elevators, water pressure, package theft, pest control, etc.

---

## E. Cat-specific

28. Is there a pet deposit, and is it refundable?
29. Is there a monthly pet rent, and how much?
30. Are there any breed or weight restrictions for cats specifically?
31. Is there an outdoor space cats can access, or is it interior-only?

---

## F. Red flag calibration (observe, do not ask directly)

- How long did it take to get a human on the phone when you called to schedule?
- Is the leasing agent the same person who answered when you called?
- Are hallways clean? Any odors, stains, or burned-out lights?
- Are amenities actually operational and clean, or staged and neglected?
- Does the agent give direct answers or pivot to selling points when pressed?
- Do other residents in the lobby or elevator look comfortable and unhurried?
