## 1. Verdict

**SKIP.** The single most disqualifying factor is the management collapse triggered by Sentral's takeover of the property in mid-2025. Under the prior operator (Legacy Partners), the building had a reasonable resident experience. Since Sentral assumed operations, multiple independent reviewers on ApartmentRatings describe complete operational breakdown: missing paperwork on move-in day, unresponsive staff, deteriorating cleanliness, homeless individuals accessing garages and stairwells, missing packages, and a pattern of deflection rather than resolution. The building's amenity package is strong on paper, but a luxury amenity stack run by a collapsed management team delivers none of its stated value. Until Sentral is replaced or substantively reformed at this location, this property is not worth touring.

## 4. Review Excerpts

1. **ApartmentRatings, Nov 2025, Negative.** A long-term resident described the building as having fundamentally changed under Sentral: homeless individuals living in parking structures and stairwells, a roach infestation, packages consistently going missing, and staff entering units without notice. Safety rated effectively nonexistent under the new team.

2. **ApartmentRatings, Nov 2025, Negative.** A former resident who praised the Legacy Partners era described Sentral's takeover as a "catastrophic" operational failure, including a documented case where a new tenant arrived to move in and was told management had "no record of anything" — the lease paperwork had simply disappeared.

3. **ApartmentRatings, Nov 2025, Negative.** A resident accused the property of soliciting or fabricating positive Google and Yelp reviews to offset the legitimate negative ones, and specifically called out named leasing staff as incompetent and harassing toward residents.

4. **RentCafe (undated, likely pre-2025), Mixed.** A reviewer praised the location and transit access but flagged street and neighbor noise as persistent, and noted laundry facilities were inconveniently located and frequently broken.

5. **ApartmentHomeLiving (undated, likely pre-2025), Positive.** A long-term resident described a warm community feel with neighbors of varied ages, accessible staff, and a pleasant pool area — this was written under the Legacy Partners management era and does not reflect current conditions.

## 7. Open Tour Questions

1. Which floors and exposures are courtyard-facing vs. street-facing, and what is the typical ambient noise level on a weekday evening?
2. Who is the current on-site property manager, how long have they been in the role, and what is the current maintenance SLA (stated turnaround time for requests)?
3. What is the status of the parking situation — is assigned resident parking available, and at what monthly cost?
4. Are any units in the building subject to affordable housing / income-restricted lease terms, and how does the building handle the mix of market-rate and affordable residents in shared amenity spaces?
5. What is the actual move-in process: when is the lease finalized, who holds the keys, and who is the point of contact if something goes wrong on move-in day?
